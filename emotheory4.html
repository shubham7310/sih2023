Let's Learn "Emotion" !!




This emotion is Anger
The "angry" emotion is often shown by a red or flushed face, furrowed brows, and clenched fists. It signifies feelings of frustration, irritation, or rage.Generally, anger is a natural response to distressing issues or situations — yet, it is often felt or expressed in ways that are scary, confusing or even unhealthy. As a result, we often consider anger to be a “bad” emotion, and we view its expression as destructive.
